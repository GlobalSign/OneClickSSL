OneClickSSL by GlobalSign

Fully automated SSL Secure Site activation

Instant issuance of highly secured, trusted SSL. Managing SSL, especially in hosting environments, requires a good understanding of the technology, and SSL often results in unnecessary costs through a number of technical issues.

OneClickSSL is a revolutionary SSL system which rewrites the SSL paradigm and turns the SSL Certificate lifecycle overhead into a simple, easy to deploy solution, saving website administrators time and costs, and allowing hosting companies to focus on their core business.
